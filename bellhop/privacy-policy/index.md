# Privacy Policy for Bellhop: Time Awareness

**Last updated: March 8, 2026**

Bellhop: Time Awareness ("the App") is developed and maintained by Jonathan Arndt ("I", "me").

---

## Data Collection

**Bellhop does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

The App has no internet connection, no analytics, no crash reporting service, and no advertising. It never contacts any external server.

---

## Data Stored on Your Device

All settings (notification interval, sound choice, visual preferences) are stored locally on your Mac using macOS UserDefaults. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not shared with any third party
- Can be removed by deleting the App

---

## Third-Party Services

Bellhop uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or cloud services.

---

## File Access

If you choose to import a custom sound or image file, the App reads that file on your device to play or display it. No copy is made and no file content is transmitted anywhere.

---

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If the App ever gains features that involve data collection, this policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will reflect that change.
