# Privacy Policy for Bellhop Cue

**Last updated: April 20, 2026**

Bellhop Cue ("the App") is developed and maintained by Jonathan Arndt ("I", "me").

---

## Data Collection

**Bellhop Cue does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

The App has no internet connection, no analytics, no crash reporting service, and no advertising. It never contacts any external server.

---

## Data Stored on Your Device

Bellhop Cue stores data locally on your Mac to provide its features. This includes:

- App settings such as notification interval, sound choice, visual preferences, Focus / DND preference, monitor preference, and volume, stored using macOS UserDefaults
- Optional custom sound files that you choose to import, copied into the App's local Application Support storage on your device
- Optional custom image files that you choose to import, copied into the App's local Application Support storage on your device
- Optional diagnostics exports that you choose to create, written locally to the App's container on your device

This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not shared with any third party
- Can be removed by deleting the App and its local data

---

## Third-Party Services

Bellhop Cue uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or cloud services.

---

## File Access

If you choose to import a custom sound or image file, the App reads that file on your device and stores a local copy inside the App's container so it can continue using it later. No file content is transmitted anywhere.

---

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If the App ever gains features that involve data collection, this policy will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will reflect that change.
